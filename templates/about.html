About

Re-Image is an online application to resize still images (pictures and photos). We seek to provide a simple online tool (just 3 clicks) for a quick and easy image resizing. We hope this app helps you avoid sending photos with size greater than 1 MB.

Re-Image is a small Sogtware Group Project launched in April 2021 by a software developers group.

We strongly believe in simplicity, that's why We put the word SIMPLE in the application name - "Re-Image".
